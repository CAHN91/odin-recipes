Hamburgers

Description

This is the recipe of super tasty and juicy burgers that you can make yourself

If you are into cheese and jelapenos this is the "to go" recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • Hamburgare 480g
  • Pepper jack ost
  • jelapenos
  • Hamburgarebröd
  • Gul lök
  • Hamburgaredressing
  • Senap och Ketchup
